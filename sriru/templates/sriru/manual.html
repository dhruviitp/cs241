INSTRUCTION MANUAL

For Students

  • Sudents can see the ongoing projects going in the college under various departments. They can select their choice of project and can than contact that professor by mailing him .
  • They can select their choice of project and can than contact that professor by mailing him .
  • Students can login on their own page from the login page with the id and password provided to them after registration under some project.
  • They can track the status of their project from their page .

For Professors

  • Professors can login on their home page by using their valid employee number and password .
  • Professors can request for starting of a new project .
  • Professors can also request for any new purchase he wants .
  • Professors can also add new fellows under any project.
  • Professor can also add any Co-Pi for any project under him.
  • Professor can see the details of the approved and the unapproved projects.
  • Professor can also see the details of the rejected,approved,unapproved and completed purchases.
  • Professor can also change their account password .
  • Professors can also see messages sent to him from different students.

FOR SPONSORS

  • Sponsors can login on their page using their id and password from the login page.
  • On thir page sponsors can check the status of the projects that they have sponsored .
  • Sponsors can also see the details of other unapproved projects in the institute and Sponsor the project which they like.
  • Sponsors can also change their account password.

FOR VENDORS

  • Vendors can manually apply to the admin on seeing a relase of Tendor. He has to then wait for the approval from the admin.

FOR ADMIN :

  • Admin can login to his account using the superuser link(given on the Homepage).
  • On his page Admin can see the list of approved and unapproved projects.
  • Admin can see the list of Approved and sanctioned purchases.
  • Admin can generate the Debit Advice Sheet.
  • Admin can add new PROFESSOR,STUDENT,DEPARTMENT,SPONSOR and VENDORS.
  • Admin can also read the messgaes sent to him from different vendors.
  • Admin can also change his account password.

FOR SRIRU APPROVER

  • SRIRU Approver can login to his page from the superuser link (given on the HOMEPAGE).
  • On his page ,SRIRU Approver can approve the unapproved projects and purchases.
  • SRIRU Approver can also change his account password.
